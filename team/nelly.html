Belgian Draft Horse

The Belgian Draft Horse is a horse breed in Red Dead Redemption 2. Both the Blond Chestnut and Mealy Chestnut can be acquired by stealing, though Belgians tend to have a low base value ($120). Belgians tend to be found pulling wagons, and are thus big, slow, and the least agile of all the horses.

Appaloosa

The Appaloosa is a horse breed in Red Dead Redemption 2. Half of the coat types will need to be tamed from the wild. Appaloosas tend to have higher stamina, average health, and speed, but lower acceleration. Its handling gives it moderate reactivity and agility.

Kentucky Saddler

The Kentucky Saddler is a horse breed in Red Dead Redemption 2. Most coat types can either be tamed or stolen, though the Grey coat can be found at the Valentine stable. Other coat types include the Chestnut Pinto, Black, and Silver Bay. The Kentucky Saddler has a low base value, making it a fairly cheap riding horse ($50). Kentucky Saddlers are very common, with low attributes. Its handling gives it moderate reactivity and agility.
